Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

AI-Empowered Solutions ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of personal information we collect through ai-empoweredsolutions.com (the "Site") and in the course of our business. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and disclose it, and the choices and rights available to you. This Policy is intended to comply with Alberta's Personal Information Protection Act, S.A. 2003, c. P-6.5 ("PIPA").

1. Who We Are

AI-Empowered Solutions is based in Alberta, Canada. If you have questions about this Policy or our privacy practices, contact our designated Privacy Officer:

Bryanne Biegel, Founder & Privacy Officer

Email: bryanne@ai-empoweredsolutions.com

2. Personal Information We Collect

We collect personal information directly from you when you interact with our Site or book time with us. The personal information we collect is limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Any other information you choose to include in the content of your message (contact form) or scheduling request (Calendly)

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and we do not collect sensitive categories of personal information (such as financial, health, or government identification numbers) through the Site.

3. Why We Collect Your Personal Information

We collect personal information only for purposes that a reasonable person would consider appropriate in the circumstances, and only to the extent necessary for those purposes. We collect and use your name and email address to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, questions, or requests submitted through the contact form
  • Schedule and confirm discovery calls and meetings
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Maintain records of our correspondence with you

At the point of collection, we will identify the purpose for collecting your personal information, either verbally or in writing (including through this Policy), unless the purpose is already reasonably apparent to you.

4. Consent

By submitting your name and email address through our contact form or a scheduling request, you consent to our collection, use, and disclosure of that information as described in this Policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time, subject to legal or contractual restrictions and reasonable notice, by contacting our Privacy Officer using the details in Section 1. If you withdraw consent, we may not be able to respond to your inquiry, book a meeting with you, or continue providing certain services to you.

5. How We Use and Disclose Your Personal Information, Including Our Service Providers

We use a limited number of service providers to help us operate our business and respond to inquiries efficiently:

Anthropic, PBC ("Anthropic")

Emails you send us (including through our contact form) may be forwarded to or synced with Claude Team, an artificial intelligence workspace product, which we use to summarize, organize, and triage correspondence.

Microsoft Corporation ("Microsoft")

We use Microsoft 365 to host our business email and to store and manage documents and records, which may include your personal information.

Calendly, Inc. ("Calendly")

When you book a discovery call or meeting with us, Calendly collects your name, email address, and any details you provide in the scheduling form on our behalf.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not disclose it to third parties for their own marketing purposes. We disclose personal information only:

  • To Anthropic, Microsoft, and Calendly, as our service providers, for the purposes described above
  • Where required or authorized by law (for example, in response to a valid legal process)
  • With your consent
